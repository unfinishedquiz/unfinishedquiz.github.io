About UnfinishedQuiz

UnfinishedQuiz is a challenging game with 50 different questions. All questions have a mathematical and logical basis which means that they are not tricky questions.

You will earn one point for each correctly answered question. Once you feel you can't go ahead because you cannot figure out the answer, simply click on "I give up" button right above the question (top right corner of the question card) to get your result. You will be asked to fill out a form but it is optional. Data you entered in the form will be used to rank you in the leader board.

Your rank on the leaderboard is calculated using number of points you earned.

If you didn't play yet, go ahead and finish the unfinished quiz. Good Luck!!!
