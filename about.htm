About UnfinishedQuiz

UnfinishedQuiz is a challenging game with 50 different questions. All questions have a mathematical basis which means that they are not trick questions.

When completing questions, you will earn one point for each. Once you feel you can't go ahead because you cannot figure out the answer, simply click on "I give up" button right above the question (top right corner of the question card). You'll ask to fill out a form but it is optional. Data you entered in the form will use to rank you in the leader board.

Your rank on the leaderboard is calculated using the amount of the score you earned.

If you didn't play yet, go ahead and finish the unfinished quiz. Good Luck!!!
